Our Story

Born from
passion,
served with pride.

Just Dosa was founded with one simple goal — to bring the authentic flavours of South India to Millpark, Melbourne. A group of friends who couldn't find the food they loved back home decided to create it themselves.

We travelled across South India tasting, discovering, and perfecting. Every dosa is crafted from scratch with fermented batter, fresh fillings, and house-made chutneys.

The People Behind Just Dosa

A message from our family

Built on a shared dream — to share the food we grew up loving with Australia.

CB

Chandra Bharath Suryababu

Chef & Co-Owner

Growing up, I always loved my mum's cooking — I've been a big foodie my whole life. When creating this menu, we travelled across South India to find the best dishes we knew would please everyone. We created Just Dosa because our group of friends couldn't find good South Indian food nearby.

SK

Santhana Krishnan

Chef & Co-Founder

For me, it's always been about the Open Benne Masala Dosa — golden-crisp, lathered in butter, with flavours you simply can't find anywhere else. That dish is what inspired us to build Just Dosa — to create a place where food this good could finally have a home in Australia.
